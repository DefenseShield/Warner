# WarFairy Arms Nephilim AR-10

#### Version
1.0

#### NOTE
THIS IS AN AR-10. THIS WILL NOT WORK WITH STANDARD AR-15 UPPER RECEIVERS OR LOWER PARTS KITS. DO YOUR RESEARCH BEFORE BUILDING.

#### Instructions 
Print all parts in the orientation supplied in the STL files. Support material is recommended for the front and rear sections. It is NOT recommended for the Tower part to preserve the printed threads.

Glue all parts with Weld-on #4 or other appropriate ABS glue. Allow glue to cure for at least 24 hours before test firing.

#### Disclaimer
THIS IS UNTESTED. USE CAUTION WHEN TEST FIRING.
